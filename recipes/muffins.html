Pumpkin muffins

Recipe description:

This is a recipe for pumpkin muffins.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups self-rising flour
  • 2 ½ cups white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
  • ⅔ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 350 deg F
  2. Mix ingredients all together except sugar and cinnamon
  3. Pour into muffin tins and dust tops with sugar and cinnamon
  4. Bake for 20 mins and enjoy!
